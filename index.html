AP U.S. Government and Politics Course Description

Introductory college-level course in U.S. government and politics focused on constitutionalism, liberty and order, civic participation, policymaking interests, and political analysis.

SectionFormatWeight
Section I55 MCQ • 1 hr 20 min • individual and set-based questions50%
Section II4 FRQs • 1 hr 40 min: Concept Application, Quantitative Analysis, SCOTUS Comparison, Argument Essay50%
